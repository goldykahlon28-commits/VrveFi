VrveFi — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 June 2026

VrveFi ("the app") is a personal-finance tool that helps you track expenses, manage budgets, follow stock prices, and get on-device money insights. This policy explains what data the app handles.

1. Data stored on your device

Your financial data — transactions, budgets, savings goals, watchlist, and settings — is stored locally on your device (in the app's local storage). It is not uploaded to us, and we have no account system and no server-side database of your finances. Removing the app removes this data.

2. Stock market data

When you view the Market screen or search for a stock, the app requests public price data from Yahoo Finance through our server proxy. These requests contain only the stock symbols being viewed — never your personal or financial data.

3. AI assistant (optional)

If the operator of a VrveFi deployment has enabled the AI assistant, the messages you send to it — together with a snapshot of your in-app financial summary used to answer your question — are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a response. This feature is optional; when it is not enabled, the assistant runs entirely on-device and sends nothing externally. See Anthropic's privacy practices at anthropic.com/legal/privacy.

4. What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not use advertising or third-party analytics/tracking SDKs.
  • We do not collect your location, contacts, or device identifiers.

5. Not financial advice

Market prices, charts, and any insights in VrveFi are provided for information only and do not constitute financial, investment, or trading advice.

6. Children

VrveFi is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

7. Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
