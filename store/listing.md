## Full description (max 4000 chars)
VrveFi is a clean, private personal-finance workspace that helps you understand
your money at a glance — and it runs right on your device.

TRACK EVERY EXPENSE
• Add income and expenses in seconds with smart auto-categorization
• Search and filter your full transaction history by category, type and month
• See where your money actually goes with clear category breakdowns

STAY ON BUDGET
• Set a budget for each category and watch live progress bars
• Get a built-in budget coach that flags overruns and suggests adjustments

REACH YOUR SAVINGS GOAL
• Set a savings goal and track your progress toward it
• See your monthly net savings, savings rate and a projected goal date

LIVE STOCK MARKET
• Search and follow ANY stock — Apple, Amazon, Netflix and thousands more
• Live prices with 24h and 7-day moves and trend sparklines
• Build a personal watchlist that's saved on your device

AI MONEY ASSISTANT
• Ask plain-English questions about your spending, budgets and savings
• Get instant, data-grounded answers — no spreadsheets required

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
• Your financial data is stored on your device — no account required
• No ads, no third-party tracking, no selling your data

Market prices and insights are provided for information only and are NOT financial
advice. Always do your own research before making financial decisions.
